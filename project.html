ABOUT THIS PROJECT

The "Nearby Vehicle Service Selection System Using Location Data" is designed to provide users with quick and efficient access to vehicle-related services within their immediate vicinity. The system leverages real-time location data to identify and display nearby service options — including repair shops, maintenance centers, and towing services — tailored to the user's current needs and location. Users can filter services by type, rating, and distance to make informed choices. The project also integrates user feedback and reviews to maintain a reliable, user-focused service selection system.
